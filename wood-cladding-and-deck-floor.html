Wall Cladding and Deck Floor

Cladding is the application of one material over another to provide skin or layer intended to control the infiltration of weather elements, or for aesthetic purposes.It is mainly done for protecting the exteriors or for increasing the aesthetic value of the walls.This in turns helps increase the durability of the external walls. It also helps in preventing noise.Hardwood cladding is the most preferred type of cladding. IPE hardwood cladding is popular in India. BVG offers affordable,good quality IPE cladding material.
